About AURA

AURA is an AI-powered seizure risk forecasting system designed for people with epilepsy. It integrates real-time data from wearable devices, sleep EEG, and smartphones to generate personalized risk predictions.

How It Works

AURA continuously collects data from smartwatches, sleep EEG headbands, and smartphones. Our cloud AI processes this information to predict seizure risk and provide insights.

Why It Matters

Predictive insights empower individuals and clinicians to make proactive decisions, improve safety, and enhance quality of life.

Our Mission

To enable better epilepsy management through AI, empowering users with personalized risk awareness and peace of mind.
